How we work

Our 4-step design process

From discovery to handoff — a structured process that's kept us on-time for 95% of our projects.

01 — DISCOVER
Research & Strategy

User interviews, competitive analysis, and product audit. We align design goals with business metrics before a single pixel is placed.

02 — DEFINE
Information Architecture

User flows, sitemaps, and interaction models. We map every path so the final product feels intuitive — not learned.

03 — DESIGN
UI & Prototyping

High-fidelity Figma screens with a full design system. Interactive prototypes for stakeholder sign-off before dev handoff.

04 — DELIVER
Handoff & Support

Developer-ready specs, asset exports, and a design system doc. We stay available during implementation to answer every edge case.
